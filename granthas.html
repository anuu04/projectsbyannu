Mobile App – Reading & Learning Experience

Grantha’s — Reviving Ancient Literature Digitally

Grantha’s is a unique mobile app designed to make ancient Indian scriptures accessible and engaging for modern readers. It combines rich cultural aesthetics with contemporary UI/UX design principles to create an immersive reading experience for sacred texts like the Ramayana and Mahabharata. The app focuses on simplifying complex scriptures with translations, explanations, and personalized features for deeper spiritual learning.

UI/UX DesignMobile AppVisual Design

Project Overview

  • Problem Statement

    Most digital scripture platforms present texts in outdated or difficult-to-navigate layouts. Users struggle with readability, lack of translation support, and minimal motivation to continue reading. This creates a disconnect between modern users and ancient wisdom.

  • Solution

    Grantha’s reimagines scripture reading through clean typography, dark-mode aesthetics, and interactive experiences. Users can explore key verses with guided explanations, track progress via dashboards, earn badges for milestones, and share favorite quotes — making the experience engaging and rewarding while preserving authenticity.

  • Impact

    ● Improved user engagement through gamified achievements.

    ● Enhanced reading comprehension with in-app translations and highlights.

    ● Revived interest in ancient literature among younger audiences through intuitive UI and storytelling-driven design.

Responsibilities

  • User Research

    Studied reader behavior and preferences for long-form religious and literature content.

  • UX Strategy

    Created a clear reading flow, from scripture selection to progress tracking.

  • UI Design

    Blended traditional Indian motifs with minimal modern visuals for a calm, immersive experience.

  • Prototype & Testing

    Conducted usability tests to ensure readability, accessibility, and ease of navigation.

1. Onboarding Flow

Introduces users to the essence of Granthas with visually immersive onboarding screens. Each screen highlights the platform’s purpose — to discover ancient wisdom, curate personalized knowledge journeys, and share insights with the world.

The visuals and microcopy together set a calm, devotional tone that connects emotionally with the audience.

Grantha’s - Onboarding Flow

2. Login Screens

Simple and visually appealing login and signup screens for a seamless user experience. Minimal form fields and clear hierarchy ensure quick onboarding.

Options for Google, Apple, and phone login add flexibility and accessibility for all user types.

Grantha’s - Login Screens

3. Home Screen → Read / Understand / Share Flow

The home experience features a personalized welcome message, a daily “Special Granthas” carousel, and a curated list of books for exploration.

Each scripture page provides interactive reading sections, including verse explanations and translations to promote better understanding.

Users can highlight and share their favorite lines or verses directly to social platforms, making engagement both spiritual and social.

Grantha’s - Home Screen To Read Flow

4. Search / Categories Flow

A clean, intuitive flow that includes a universal search bar and categorized collections for easy exploration. Users can browse through topics like “Vedic Literature” or “Bhagavad Gita” and quickly access relevant scriptures.

The layout ensures fast navigation and instant recognition through cover visuals.

Grantha’s - Search/Categories Flow

5. Watch Podcast Flow

This section allows users to listen to and watch podcasts on various Granthas and spiritual topics. Featuring horizontal carousels and curated playlists, users can explore trending episodes.

Upon selection, they are redirected to YouTube for a seamless listening and viewing experience.

Grantha’s - Watch Podcast Flow

6. Profile Flow

The profile section empowers users to track their reading journey and spiritual growth. It includes stats such as completed chapters, bookmarked verses, and downloaded texts.

Users also earn badges for milestones — encouraging consistency and motivation. Progress tracking adds a sense of achievement and personalization.
